We've Merged Into My Fence Guys

About The Merge

My Fence Guys is a combination of the same friendly staffs from Northern Virginia Fence Company and Fairfax Fence Company.

With a combined 25 + years in the industry, these two dedicated companies decided it was time to combine forces to provide an even better customer experience.

Some of the benefits of this merger include: lower overhead costs that result if savings that are passed through to our customers, faster response time to both initial and follow-on requests, and lower material costs resulting in more savings that we also pass through.
