About My Fence Guys

My Fence Guys was formed out of three construction firms. Our founders bring 35 years of combined experience to our work on residential fences in the Manassas and surrounding northern Virginia area. Our local, veteran-owned company is licensed and insured. We'll work hard to exceed your expectations.

My Fence Guys offers:

  • 35 years of experience
  • Outstanding fencing products by Trex and other leading brands
